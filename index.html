Ariyo Imanuel Tarigan
PhD student at University of the Ryukyus

I’m currently working in coral taxonomy, with a special interest in how hybridization and introgression shape relationships among coral species. I study gene flow, adaptation, and gamete recognition during spawning, aiming to understand how species boundaries are maintained—or crossed—in nature.
